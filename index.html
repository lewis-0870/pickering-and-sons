Welcome to Pickering & Sons Building Contractors

We are a family-run building contractor business based in Redcar, North Yorkshire. With over 10 years of experience in the construction industry, we pride ourselves on delivering high-quality workmanship and exceptional customer service.

Our team of skilled professionals specializes in a wide range of services, including new builds, renovations, extensions, and general repairs. We work closely with our clients to understand their needs and provide tailored solutions that meet their requirements and budget.

Premium Garden Room Construction in Redcar

×

This project showcases a custom-built, blockwork garden room recently completed for a customer in Redcar. Our team focused on durability and a high-quality finish, delivering a structure designed for year-round utility and longevity.

• Robust Blockwork Shell: The structure was constructed using high-density blockwork, providing superior structural integrity and excellent thermal mass compared to traditional timber framing.

• High-End Weatherproofing: The roof features a durable EPDM (ethylene propylene diene monomer) membrane , ensuring a completely watertight, low-maintenance seal that is built to last for decades.

• Professional Render Finish: The exterior was expertly finished with a smooth, sand-cement render , providing a clean, aesthetic appeal and an added layer of weather protection. This custom finish elevates the appearance far beyond a standard shed.

• Modern Glazing Solutions: The installation includes premium uPVC French doors and accompanying uPVC window units, offering maximum natural light and easy access, complete with modern seals and hardware.

This completed building is a testament to our commitment to detailed craftsmanship and delivering functional, aesthetically pleasing outdoor spaces for our clients. Another successful project checked off!
